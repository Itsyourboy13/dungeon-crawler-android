How to Install & Play

Requirements:

  • Android 12+ (API 31+)
  • 50MB free space
  • Allow "Install from unknown sources" in settings

Installation:

  1. Download the APK from above.
  2. Open the file on your device.
  3. Follow prompts to install.

How to Play:

  • Home Screen: View high/recent scores. Tap "Start Game" or "Help".
  • Gameplay: Use directional buttons to move (blue circle player). Battles auto-start with monsters (red circles).
  • Monitor HP (green) and XP (yellow). Use potions (up to 4) to heal.
  • Pinch to zoom (1x-3x), drag to pan.
  • Win levels by clearing monsters. Game over if HP=0 â€” submit score.
  • Tap screen to toggle UI (auto-hides).

Tips:

  • Plan moves to avoid multiple monsters.
  • Save potions for low HP.
  • Level up for better stats and more potions.
